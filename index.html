How to use

Never used AI prompts before? Here's exactly what to do.

A "prompt" is just what you type into ChatGPT or any AI tool. The more specific you are, the better the result. These prompts are already written the right way — you just fill in your own details.

01

Pick a category above

Choose the topic that matches what you are trying to do — images, resume, job search, or salary.

02

Find a prompt that fits your situation

Scroll through the list. Each prompt has a short note explaining when to use it. Pick the one closest to what you need.

03

Copy it — one click on the code block

Click the copy button on the code block. The full prompt is now on your clipboard.

04

Paste into ChatGPT (or any AI tool) and fill in the blanks

Open ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini. Paste the prompt. Replace anything inside [brackets] with your real information. Hit send.

05

Want even better results?

Use PromptIt — paste your rough idea and it automatically rewrites it into a stronger, more detailed prompt before you run it.
